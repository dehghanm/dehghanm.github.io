My full name is "HosseinAli Rahmani Dashti", and my nickname is "Saeed". I prefer to be called "Saeed" and generally everyone knows me by my nickname. But in academic and papers, I use the abbreviated "Hossein A. Rahmani" of my full name.
I am a Visiting Researcher at Information Retrival group at the Università della Svizzera italiana (USI), working on information retrieval (IR) and recommender systems (RecSys) problems. Prior to that, I participated in the European Union project consists of Vienna University of Technology, University of Bonn, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki and two European SMEs focused on Data Science and Big Data. I am also a master's student at the Multi Agent Systems Lab MASlab at the University of Zanjan (ZNU), under supervision of Prof. Mohsen Afsharchi and Dr. Mitra Baratchi (co-supervisor from Leiden University). I received my B.Sc. degree from the University of Zanjan.
